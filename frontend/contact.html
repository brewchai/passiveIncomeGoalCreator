Frequently Asked Questions

Is But First Fire really free?

Yes! Our core FIRE calculator and planning tools are completely free to use. We believe everyone should have access to quality financial planning tools.

Can I get personalized financial advice?

While our tools provide helpful projections and AI-powered tips, we don't provide personalized financial advice. For specific guidance, we recommend consulting with a licensed financial advisor.

How accurate are the retirement projections?

Our projections are based on standard financial assumptions (like the 4% rule and historical market returns). They're meant to give you a realistic estimate, but actual results will vary based on market conditions and your specific situation.
